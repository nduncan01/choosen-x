Welcome to Chosen-X Speaker

Chosen-X was invented by a young Male that attends Johnson & Wales University in Providence, Rhode Island. This young male goes by the name of Nicoral Duncan. Nicoral always enjoyed listening to music but after he bought so many speakers within his lifetime, he decided to create a speaker of his own. This speaker included features such as, voice assistance, Bluetooth, aux, and great sound quality. Choosen-X has yet to accomplish all of our goals but we are off to a great start to accomplish many.

Voice Assistance

When creating the voice assistance in this speaker, the team of Chosen-X speaker wanted to do something different then all of our other competitors. The voice assistance is named X and in order to set it up you push the voice assistance button on the speaker, the speaker will then give you a list of direction such as saying X multiple times so the speaker can remember your voice.

Bluetooth

Chosen- X Speaker can connect to any device that has Bluetooth, such as cell phones, iPod, iPad, and computers. In order to connect the device to the Chosen-X speaker the two must be within 15ft from one another.

Powerful Sound

When listening to Chosen-X speaker we want to provide a powerful and healthy sound quality. Sound quality is very important to our company because we want our customers to love our product. When using our product quiet is not an option, when watching a movie, we want our customers to feel like they are in a movie theater, and when listening to music they feel like they are at a concert.

Aux

When purchasing a speaker it come with a aux cord, this is another way that you could connect your phone or device to the speaker but when you playing music or any type of audio, the device must stay connect with the cord at all times or you will not be able to play audio. The aux is located on the back of the speaker
